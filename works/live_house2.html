Live House 2.0
digital art, 2015
Live house 2.0 是一個音樂互動空間，是延續 Live House 的作品， 我們將視覺以及控制器進行了改良，期望達到更加直覺的互動效果。

我們自製了幾個大型的控制器， 當觀者操控了我們的大型樂器， 空間中會產生音效以及對應的視覺效果。

我們希望參與的人可以像孩子一樣自在地玩耍， 自由的創作自己的音樂， 同時我們也希望挑戰音樂給予人們的感官感受， 我們認為音樂不單只會對聽覺形成刺激， 搭配上相對應的視覺及觸覺，更能讓人感受音樂的力量。
Live House 2.0 is a music interactive space. It is a revision work of Live House. We improve the visual program and controller. We expect to achieve a more intuitive interaction effect.

We made a couple of large controllers, and build a space surrounded by the projection for viewer to feel the music. When the viewer manipulate our large musical instruments, the space will surrounded by music and the corresponding visual effects.

We hoped people can participate freely like a child. and freely create their music. We also wanted to challenge the feeling of music. We believe that music is not only about hearing. With the corresponding visual and tactile, people can feel the more power of music.
